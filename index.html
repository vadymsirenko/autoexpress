Why import cars from the USA?

We compared the US markets with European ones and realized that purchasing a car in America is several times more profitable, no matter how paradoxical it may sound. This is due to well-thought-out logistics, the level of development of services for evaluating the condition of cars, and the purchasing process itself.

Most US citizens lease a car for several years, and throughout the lease period, the leasing company takes care of regular maintenance, making cars from the USA one of the best choices for car enthusiasts in Ukraine.

Why such a low price?

Damaged cars from the USA are purchased at insurance company auctions. At these auctions, a car can lose half its value even due to minimal damage. Considering the monetary costs, such as purchase, delivery, customs, and repair, the price of a similar car in Ukraine will be 35-50% higher, while new ones will cost astronomical amounts.
